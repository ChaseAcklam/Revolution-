Most small and medium size shippers do not have the time, resources, or capital to implement a capable Transportation Management System (TMS). Revolution has solved that issue by developing an award-winning TMS solution called RPM. RPM is a cloud-based solution that allows shippers to manage their truckload, LTL, and parcel all in one place. You will have access to more than 1,400 carriers directly and can load your own rates and/or check the spot market to secure “daily specials” for the best overall rate and service.

Not only will we implement this solution free of charge, but you will be up and running in under 4 weeks. We’ve helped demanding shippers leapfrog their competition while saving hundreds of thousands of dollars in implementation and maintenance fees. Better yet, you will be able to conduct freight-bill and audit with the push of a button.
